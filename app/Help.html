How it works

Be a part of this amazing community by following simple steps! Just make an account with us, registering as a parent looking for a professional or a nanny willing to share your experience on child care. Once making your account nannies and parents will be able to check on your profiles and connect between them.

FAQs

Creating your profile is so simple! Click on the “sign up” button on the main page and enter you information, mostly for contact and for community to know more about you as parent or nanny. Follow the sign up steps once clicking on the button.
You don’t need any certification as a nanny for be part of the team; Nevertheless, some positions and availabilities may need you to have certain certifications, that can be arranged directly with the parents/employer.
Payments must be arranged directly with the nanny providing the service. You’ll be able to consult the rate and total payment for nanny services on our webpage.
You’ll be able to consult nanny or parents references directly on nannies’ / parents profiles, every time you select a profile to be consulted, you’ll be able to see previous experiences with other parents as your reference, You can, also, ask directly for personal references to nanny or parents.
